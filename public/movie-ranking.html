The Best Movies According to Audrey

My top 3 movies of all time

The Intouchables

This is my favourite film of all-time. A beautiful story about friendship and pain and with elements of poverty and racism.

Chicago

This is my go-to when I need something exciting and riveting but easy to watch. Catherine Zeta-Jones is a knockout in this film.

The Shawshank Redemption

This is a classic with great cinematography and writing.
